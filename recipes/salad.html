Salad

Description

Salad is nothing but a mixture of cut fruits and/or vegetables.

The ingredients needed to prepare this dish can be found across the globe and it doesn't take more than 10 minutes to make enough Salad for a healthy breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Fruits
  • Vegetables
  • Salad Dressings (optional)

Steps

  1. Cut fruits and vegetables
  2. Add salad dressings as per your convenience
