Tea

Description

Tea is nothing but chemicals extracted from leaves in hot water.

The ingredients needed to prepare this dish can be found across the globe and it doesn't take more than 10 minutes to make enough Tea for a healthy breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Tea leaves
  • Water
  • Sugar (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Heat tea leaves in boiled water for two-five minutes depending on the desired strength
  3. Add sugar as per your convenience
