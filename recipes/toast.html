Toast

Description

Toast is nothing but loaves of bread that have been heated to a perfect crisp.

The ingredients needed to prepare this dish can be found across the globe and it doesn't take more than 10 minutes to make enough Toast for a healthy breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Loaves of bread
  • Butter (optional)
  • Cheese (optional)
  • Honey (optional)
  • Namkeen (optional)

Steps

  1. Place loaves of bread in a Toaster
  2. Heat loaves for two-five minutes depending on the desired crisp
  3. Apply Butter/Cheese/Honey/Namkeen as per your convenience
